Vehicle

A vehicle (from Latin: vehiculum [1]) is a machine that transports people or cargo. Vehicles include:

  • Wagons
  • Bicycles
  • Motor vehicles (motorcycles, cars, trucks, buses)
  • Railed vehicles (trains, trams)
  • Watercraft (ships, boats)
  • Amphibious vehicles (screw-propelled vehicle, hovercraft)
  • Aircraft (airplanes, helicopters)
  • Spacecraft

Land vehicles are classified broadly by what is used to apply steering and drive forces against the ground:

  • Wheeled
  • Tracked
  • Railed
  • Skied

ISO 3833-1977 is the standard, also internationally used in legislation, for road vehicles types, terms and definitions.
